Who Am I?

I'm currently a Computer Engineering Student who's absolutely thrilled by all things tech. My days are spent diving deep into the world of circuits, algorithms, and software. But that's not all - I'm also a passionate Discord Bot Developer. I love putting my coding skills to work, creating bots that add a spark of interactivity to online communities. It's like breathing life into lines of code!

On top of that, I take on the role of Trainings and Workshop Director. Sharing knowledge is a big deal for me, so I organize and lead various sessions where I get to pass on what I know. It's incredibly fulfilling to see fellow students and colleagues light up with understanding as I break down complex topics. I guess you could say I'm all about empowering others to embrace the tech world.

So yeah, in a nutshell, I'm a tech enthusiast who's juggling being a Computer Engineering Student, a Discord Bot Developer, and a Trainings and Workshop Director. It's a busy life, but I wouldn't have it any other way!
